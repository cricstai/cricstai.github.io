About us

The year 2018 began with Virushka trending on social media, and absolutely it has been a great delight for any cricket or cinema buff. And, the disclaimer begins here, the focus of this piece of writing is not about discussing this celebrity wedding but the evergreen game of cricket keeping the dazzle intact. This Gentlemen’s game is one among the forms of sport played with a stick and a ball, along with Hockey and Baseball. The game comprising of two teams which perform two switching roles; one team bats first and try to score runs, while other team bowl and fields. With a fan base of over 2 billion, it is the second most popular game following football. The main reason for the popularity of the game is the impressive performance of home teams in the international arena.

Cricket may come as outlandish to many, due to several reasons and the inevitable challenges for any person living a usual life. We have the power to dream and aspire and make things happen. Cricsta India believes in the similar power of dreams and passion with a strategic focus. Cricsta India is a cricket marketing and scouting platform for serious cricketers who believe in their talent to be the next superstars of the game. If you have the rights ingredients to be a Cricket professional, the challenges will not remain challenges, because you get a helping hand, you get the right support and guidance and at the right time. How does Cricsta India, function?

· Connect and Create: It is a body for promoting and developing cricket on every level in India. It helps budding cricketers to connect with fellow cricketers, clubs and their coaches. By being connected to the professionals, one gets an opportunity to learn and improve their experiences, which helps them dwell as professionals themselves.

· Tackling Institutional Challenges: There is a dearth of professional support to help talents grow and due to these challenges, the actual talents miss upon these opportunities. We at Cricsta work towards these issues by providing: 1) Access to best training, coaching and competition 2) Facilitate coaching platform, coaching education 3) Scouting social media platform, to display and connect with other professionals

· Pacing with Professional Changes: Keeping a track of recent trends and developments in the arena of cricket, we don’t want anyone to lose these opportunities. One of the focus- New Player Initiative promotes benefits of professional cricket to upcoming players and provide them with professional acumen.

· Developing World Class Professionals: The focus lies upon connecting and creating, also we value the importance of a world-class professional development of the players. The ever-shrinking global arena and with the expansion of global standards, Cricsta India focuses upon dwelling with international level of professional standards.
